Who we are

A new and unlike anything in its format institution in an urban cultural center. The Bachelor's Bar has absorbed years of travel around the world, learning about the features of street food and gourmet cuisine. It took a long time to combine simple and complex and add a pint of cold and refreshing beer to it all. We have collected the best intoxicants from around the world to quench the thirst of even the most demanding guest. The Bachelor's Bar pours delicious beer, prepares extraordinary dishes, and sets the mood.
