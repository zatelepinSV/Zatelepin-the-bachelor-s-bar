Who we are

The Bachelor's Bar is a new unique venue in an urban cultural center. We have absorbed years of travel around the world, learning about the features of street food and gourmet cuisine. It took us a long time to combine simple and complex and add a pint of cold refreshing beer to it all. We have collected the best intoxicants from around the world to quench the thirst of even the most demanding guest. The Bachelor's Bar pours delicious beer, prepares extraordinary dishes and sets the mood.

Visit us

An excellent establishment is the harmony of many aspects such as interior, food and drinks, music, average check and of course the staff. We have assembled a team of people who share our views because only a cheerful and contented team will create coziness which you won't find everywhere.
