EXQUISITE COCTIAL BAR

Traditional ideas, contemporary execution.

Each of our three floors offer a distinctly different experience. For bottled punch, mixed drinks and lively service, visit the Taproom. For a cocktail menu like no other, join us in the Parlor. For private events, The Occasional Room is available for up to 60 guests. Better yet, visit all three.

Fear not. Our food menu, famed Irish Coffee and Irish Whiskey list are available on all floors.

THE TAPROOM

GREAT FOOD, GREAT SERVICE, GREAT CRAIC.

Our Taproom is a true celebration of that greatest Irish institution, the pub. Traditional Irish pub fare made with the best ingredients is served alongside tavern-inspired cocktails, bottled punch, pints of plain and our famed Irish Coffee. Every Sunday we host a live Irish seisiún with which to end the week. Whiskey lovers - rejoice. Our back bar will leave you spoilt for choice. So, pull up a pew, what’ll it be?
